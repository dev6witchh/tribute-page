What Makes Stan Lee An EXtraOrdinary Human

  • He co-created Spider-Man, Iron Man, Fanatstic Four, Thor, the Avengers, Hulk, X-Men and many others.

  • He's worked at Marvel Comics since he was 17 years old.

  • He served in the army as playwright for three years.

  • He's now worth 200 million dollars.

  • He's married to his wife for more than 60 years now.

  • He has his own foundation, the Stan Lee Foundation.

  • He has appeared in many movies in cameo roles.

  • He was instrumental in the revision of the Comics Code.

  • He was inducted into the Will Eisner Award Hall of Fame and the Jack Kirby Hall of Fame.

  • He has received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and the Vanguard Award from the Producers Guild of America.
